The Hour of Code

The Mission and First Step

In hour-of-code we should teach codding to 20 children who are don't have any information about it. Our first mission was to find 20 participants. Instructors advised us to find students from neighborhood but we could not find them, that is why we went to the course which included 5-9 class students and they learn Azerbaijani languge so they did not know any information about our topic. After finding children we should have provided them with the needed equipment, so we went to the school where previously Davud was studying.

The Second Step

In class firstly we explained to children what is our aim, then Sabir and Davud started the presentation about ADA university and persuaded children to come to ADA. Their eyes were full of enthusiasm and with every new piece of information about the university they were getting more involved.

The Third Step

Davud provided the basic information to participants about codding. Then the whole class started working.

The Fourth Step

Sabir,Aziz, and Samira dealed with children individually and Davud worked with the rest of them because there were 20 computers for 26 participants.

Problems

  • Certificates: We had 20 certificates but then we learned that there were 26 children. After the lesson we printed 6 certificates and presented them to the rest of participants.

  • Childrens' noises: They could not adapt to the lesson and were making a lot of voice but then we managed it all by telling them that this way we will not be able to teach them anything.
